Kinetic Energy Lab
In this lab you will use your fire extinguisher to give different amounts of energy to astronaut Sandra. You can control the mass of Sandra, the force from her extinguisher, and the distance over which the extinguisher will be firing. You can also limit the energy given to her by the fire extinguisher or set a maximum speed. The fire extinguisher will automatically shut off when it is out of energy or when you reach maximum speed.

When you are ready to start the experiment, click on the begin button.
